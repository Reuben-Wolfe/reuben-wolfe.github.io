Evaluation of web graphics

Site evaluated: Valve

Purpose of the graphics on this site:

The graphics on the page are the first thing you see when you first open the page. The purpose of these graphics are too get your attention and display the products that they sell. They catch your eye and the shifting images keeps your eye drawn to the head of the page. Though this is annoying at times, there is not much text on the index page.

Guidelines for effective use of graphics:

  1. Yes - Graphics fit the purpose, organization, and syle of the page.
  2. No - There are large graphics on the page.
  3. Yes - graphics help guide users attention to the important information on the page.
  4. Yes - Overyly bright graphics have been avoided.
  5. Yes - The graphics have not been used for textual context.
  6. Yes - Alternate text is present for all images.
  7. Yes - Where text appears in graphics (the banner) there is ample contrast.
